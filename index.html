ALAN is a secure, private, and localized cloud extension which provides for the centralization of your life, without the threat of compromising your security or privacy. As an extension of The Cloud, ALAN keeps your data synchronized, centralized, and easily accessible while remaining a private system similar to your personal computer.
ALAN is the core component of your computing ecosystem. It takes the convenience of personal computing to a new level by providing a single file system for all your devices to run on. Current devices run local operating systems, requiring for applications to be isolated to a single device and the need to sync files between devices. ALAN runs lusciOS and keeps everything together.

Better Together.

The lusciOS operating system can turn all your devices into a single, visibly similar, environment. Answer phone calls from your TV. Run heavy photo editing applications on your smartphone. Play console quality games on your tablet. Get access to anything you have on any device. Everything is now together on one synchronous operating system. Everything is together and runs on the same OS. Better together.

Let’s end web forms.

The Cuore Graph® is a special feature of lusciOS where all your personal information can be stored once in a specialized database with an extensible API, so application will no longer need to ask you your name. Instead, they will just ask permission to access the information. No more redundant data. Filling out FAFSA can take your mom only one click next year. Your bank decides to send you a new credit card? No problem. Just change it once in your system and your recurring payments won’t be sending you hate mail next month. Don’t want an application to have your name in their database anymore? No problem. Just deny their access to your Graph®. All the data you have shared gets revoked and backed up to your machine.

Save Battery, Save Life.

One of the worst parts of the mobile device experience is battery life. As computing power has increased, battery life has decreased. Battery technologies are restricting the capabilities of mobile technologies. Using lusciOS special server client capabilities we have changed the mobile computing game. No more losing power after just hours of utilizing your device. Devices utilizing lusciOS and ALAN could potentially last weeks or months on one charge.

ALAN Remembers.

Every time you check out have a financial transaction ALAN records the purchase and inventories the items you bought. ALAN keeps track of your assets and allows for easy asset management.

Spend with Confidence.

ALAN has direct access to Cuore’s “Coordination Efficiency Platform” allowing it to make smart financial decisions for you. If you are going over budget he will let you know, if you want to indulge yourself in a guilty pleasure ALAN can let you know the consequences.

Automate Your Shopping

Items like groceries, gas and many common household goods requires hours of our week just to keep track of and purchase. ALAN can not only track the status of these goods, he also can plan the optimal way of purchasing them.

Bringing Businesses and Customers Closer.

No more AdWords. We provide real results connecting your business with consumers. We are so confident in our model we only charge a business once they have purchased items and are officially your customer. No more guessing how to connect via advertisements.

Everyone can listen to what they want to. unaTone also comes with Electro Permanent Magnets with Wireless charging mounts to charge your speakers. unaTone’s mount will be able to not only mount your speakers to the wall magnetically, but also simultaneously charge them. Speakers are all wireless with up to 15 hours of battery life. unaTone speakers are also all indoor/outdoor compatible. Take it wherever you need to go. Use it for whatever you need. Complete ease of use, compatibility, and professional quality. Available soon.
