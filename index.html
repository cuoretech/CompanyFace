Sync Your World

Decentralize your Data, Centralize your Life

Main Section

"We got tired of wasting our phones’ battery life, files flying everywhere, and filling out thousands of web forms just so companies could collect big data to guess the type of underwear we wear. So we created ALAN." -Leo Schultz, President

ALAN is a secure, private, and localized cloud extension which provides for the centralization of your life, without the threat of compromising your security or privacy. As an extension of The Cloud, ALAN keeps your data synchronized, centralized, and easily accessible while remaining a private system similar to your personal computer.

ALAN is the core component of your computing ecosystem. It takes the convenience of personal computing to a new level by providing a single file system for all your devices to run on. Current devices run local operating systems, requiring for applications to be isolated to a single device and the need to sync files between devices. ALAN runs lusciOS and keeps everything together.

The Cloud that’s not “The Cloud”

It’s a personal private system giving you all the functionality and conveniences of the centralized computing environment you love, without infringing on your personal life.

The brain behind “SMART” technologies

Smart technologies are growing, but they are limited by the mobile platforms and individuals’ trust in the “Cloud”. Every SmartHome needs a control center; ALAN’s platform allows for smart technology companies to use ALAN to keep their products functionality secure and exclusive to the homeowner. ALAN provides a powerful, secure and private solution to the smart network.

Not big data, Best data.

All companies love to collect data, and even government agencies have begun a trend of big data. Although collecting mass amounts of data can have a time and place, our personal lives are no place for these practices.

ALAN builds a personalized data Graph®, for all your personal information. ALAN asks you directly for your information, then companies can request access to these data fields. You can control how much data you feel comfortable sharing. No need for companies to creep on your browser or search history to see if you own a cat. Now, if you feel comfortable allowing them access to this information, they can even know your cat’s name. And the best part - you can take that information back whenever you want to.

Not just photo sharing apps.

ALAN’s platform enables specialized hybrid peer to peer applications, which allow for developers to provide personalized applications without data security being a concern. ALAN’s applications increases functionality for SmartNodes. You could even potentially have an application that controlled the locks on your door. In current models, smartphones act as the central platform for smart-enabled products. Since smartphones are limited in computing power, security, and battery life; ALAN acts as a better solution in this paradigm.

Not Siri, ALAN

ALAN gets to know you personally. Siri is limited by the amount of structured data in the Web. A very limited number of data on the web is able to be filtered directly to you. Since ALAN keeps secrets better than Jack Bauer, he can learn from you and completely personalize your devices. No frustration, only convenience.

Better Together.

The lusciOS operating system can turn all your devices into a single, visibly similar, environment. Answer phone calls from your TV. Run heavy photo editing applications on your smartphone. Play console quality games on your tablet. Get access to anything you have on any device. Everything is now together on one synchronous operating system. Everything is together and runs on the same OS. Better together.

Let’s end web forms.

The Cuore Graph® is a special feature of lusciOS where all your personal information can be stored once in a specialized database with an extensible API, so application will no longer need to ask you your name. Instead, they will just ask permission to access the information. No more redundant data. Filling out FAFSA can take your mom only one click next year. Your bank decides to send you a new credit card? No problem. Just change it once in your system and your recurring payments won’t be sending you hate mail next month. Don’t want an application to have your name in their database anymore? No problem. Just deny their access to your Graph®. All the data you have shared gets revoked and backed up to your machine.

Save Battery, Save Life.

One of the worst parts of the mobile device experience is battery life. As computing power has increased, battery life has decreased. Battery technologies are restricting the capabilities of mobile technologies. Using lusciOS special server client capabilities we have changed the mobile computing game. No more losing power after just hours of utilizing your device. Devices utilizing lusciOS and ALAN could potentially last weeks or months on one charge.
